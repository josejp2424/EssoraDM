EssoraDM changes

- Project and binary renamed to essoradm.
- Config renamed to /etc/essoradm.conf.
- Data dir renamed to /usr/share/essoradm.
- Theme file renamed to essoradm.theme.
- PAM service renamed to essoradm.
- Lock/log/auth paths renamed to essoradm.
- Adds /etc/essoradm/pre-start hook.
- pre-start hook starts /usr/local/bin/essora-splash, waits, and stops it before X/greeter.
- Original Slim GPL-2.0 license and credits are kept under /usr/share/doc/essoradm/ when packaged.
